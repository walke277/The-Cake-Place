Fun Facts About Cakes

Fun Facts About Cakes

Expand each section below to learn some fun facts about cakes!

  • Oldest

    The oldest known cake recipe dates back to ancient Rome and was called "Satura." It was a heavy, bread-like dessert made with nuts, barley, and honey, very different from the fluffy cakes we enjoy today!

  • Tallest

    The world record for the tallest cake ever made is 108 feet tall, created in 2011 in Indonesia. This incredible cake was crafted to celebrate the country's independence day and took an entire team of bakers to complete!

  • Birthdays

    The tradition of birthday cakes dates back to ancient Greece, where people would make round cakes to honor Artemis, the goddess of the moon. These cakes were decorated with candles to mimic the moonlight!
